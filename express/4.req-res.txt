Lifecylce summary -----
1. Client sends request
2. Node.js receives request
3. Express wraps req/res
4. Middleware chain starts
5. Route matched
6. Controller executes
7. Database/API calls
8. Response sent
9. Connection completes

What is next() ? --------
next() passes control to the next middleware.
